About Desi Dhaba

From Sarson Fields to Dubai Streets

Desi Dhaba brings the authentic taste of Punjab's roadside dhabas to the heart of Bur Dubai. Our story began in the mustard fields of Punjab, where generations of our family have perfected the art of traditional cooking.

Every dish tells a story - from our slow-cooked Dal Makhani that simmers overnight to our hand-rolled rotis made with love. We celebrate Punjab's rich hospitality, vibrant culture, and the warmth that makes every meal a festival.
